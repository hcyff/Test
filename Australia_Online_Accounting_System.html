How do I sign up for AOAS?


1. Go to http://www.aoas.com.au.



2. Click Sign Up.

3. Fill out the form.



4. Once you sign up, you'll need to confirm your email address. We'll send you an email to help you confirm your account.


How do I log into my AOAS account?


1. Go to http://www.aoas.com.au.

2. Enter Email address and Password.

3. Click Login.


Forgot your password?


1. Find I forget my password at the login page and click it.



2. Fill out your email address, then click OK and follow the on-screen instructions.


How do I update my company information?


1. Find the warning message after you login.



2. Click Click me to update.

3. Fill out the form.

4. Your company information will automatically show in the each electronic invoice.

How do I add an invoice?


1. Find toolbar at the top of the dashboard.

2. Click Sales.

3. Find Add invoice in the drop-down list and click it.



4. Fill the invoice form and item information.You can add new customer name, payment term and project profiles as shown below. The total price will be displayed in the lower right corner of the page.



5. Click Submit.


What should I do when receive a payment?


1. Click Sales on the toolbar

2. Find Receive Invoice Payment in the drop-down list and click it.



3. Choose a customer name.



4. Click Next Step.

5. Fill out the form as instructions and click Submit.


How do I modify or delete a created invoice?


1. Click Sales on the toolbar.



2. Find All invoices in the drop-down list and click it.

3. Modify and Delete button at the right side of the list.

4. You only can delete unpaid invoices.If you want delete paid invoices,you should delete invoice payments first. Click Sales on the toolbar,find All Invoice payments and click, the Delete button at the right side of the list.
